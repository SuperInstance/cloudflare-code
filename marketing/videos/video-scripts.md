## Video 5: Feature Spotlight - Semantic Caching (4 minutes)

**Type:** Feature Deep Dive
**Target:** Technical Audience
**Distribution:** YouTube, Blog, Social Media
**Style:** Technical, detailed, visual

### Script

**[SCENE: Technical host in studio]**

**HOST (0:00-0:30)**
"Today we're diving deep into ClaudeFlare's semantic caching - the technology that enables 94% cache hit rates and 99.7% cost reduction. This is where the magic happens."

**[VISUAL: Traditional caching diagram]**

**HOST (0:30-1:00)**
"Traditional caching only matches exact queries. Ask 'How do I create a function?' and 'How do I define a function?' - you get two cache misses. Same answer, double the cost."

**[VISUAL: Semantic caching diagram with vectors]**

**HOST (1:00-1:45)**
"ClaudeFlare uses semantic caching. We convert queries into vector embeddings using the same technology that powers ChatGPT. Similar questions have similar vectors, even if the words are different. We can match 'create a function' and 'define a function' as semantically equivalent."

**[VISUAL: 3D vector space visualization]**

**HOST (1:45-2:30)**
"We use HNSW - Hierarchical Navigable Small World - indexes for fast similarity search. Combined with 8-bit product quantization, we can store millions of vectors in memory and retrieve matches in under 10 milliseconds."

**[VISUAL: Real-time cache performance demo]**

**HOST (2:30-3:15)**
"Watch this live demo. I'll ask similar questions in different ways, and you'll see most hit the cache. The latency difference is dramatic - cached responses take 23 milliseconds versus uncached at 1.2 seconds. That's a 98% speedup."

**[VISUAL: Cache statistics dashboard]**

**HOST (3:15-4:00)**
"The results speak for themselves. Production systems see 90-94% cache hit rates. That means 90% of your AI requests cost essentially nothing and return instantly. It's not just about cost - it's about user experience. Sub-100ms responses feel instant. Over 1 second, and users notice."

**[VISUAL: Performance comparison charts]**

**HOST (4:00-end)**
"Semantic caching is table stakes for production AI applications. ClaudeFlare makes it accessible, scalable, and free. Try it yourself - the difference is immediate."

# ClaudeFlare Launch Plan

## Overview

This document outlines the comprehensive launch strategy for ClaudeFlare, positioning it as the premier AI-powered application factory built on Cloudflare's serverless infrastructure.

## Launch Objectives

### Primary Goals
- **Acquire 10,000+ users** in the first 6 months
- **Achieve 5% conversion** from free to paid plans
- **Generate $500k ARR** by end of Year 1
- **Establish ClaudeFlare** as thought leader in AI + Cloudflare space

### Secondary Goals
- Build active developer community (5,000+ members)
- Achieve 90% product-led adoption
- Secure 100+ enterprise pilot customers
- Generate positive media coverage (50+ articles)

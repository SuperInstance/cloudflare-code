# ClaudeFlare Monetization Strategy

## Executive Summary

ClaudeFlare is positioned as a premium AI-powered application factory that leverages Cloudflare's serverless infrastructure to enable rapid development and deployment of scalable applications. Our monetization strategy focuses on tiered subscription pricing with usage-based overage charges, combined with premium features and marketplace opportunities.

## Pricing Strategy

### Tiered Subscription Model

#### 1. Free Tier ($0/month)
- **Target**: Developers getting started, individual users
- **Capacity**: 3 projects, 5 team members, 1GB storage
- **Features**: Basic project templates, community support
- **Goal**: Acquire users, build product habit, convert to paid

#### 2. Pro Tier ($29/month or $290/year)
- **Target**: Growing teams, serious projects
- **Capacity**: 10 projects, 25 team members, 10GB storage
- **Features**: AI Assistant (1,000 credits/month), priority support
- **Goal**: Capture power users, generate consistent revenue

#### 3. Enterprise Tier (Custom Pricing)
- **Target**: Large organizations, enterprise deployments
- **Capacity**: Unlimited projects, unlimited team
- **Features**: Dedicated support, SSO, compliance, custom domains
- **Goal**: High-value contracts, enterprise sales

### Annual Billing Incentives
- Pro tier: Save $48 with annual billing (17% discount)
- Enterprise tier: Custom annual pricing available

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscription Revenue (Primary)
- **Free → Pro Conversion**: 5% target conversion rate
- **Pro → Enterprise**: 10% annual migration rate
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**: $350 annually

### 2. Usage-Based Revenue (Secondary)
- **AI Credits**: $5 per 2,000 credits ($0.0025/credit)
- **Storage Overage**: $0.10/GB/month
- **Compute Overage**: $0.05/hour
- **API Calls**: $0.01 per 1,000 calls

### 3. Marketplace Revenue
- **Template Marketplace**: 30% commission on premium templates
- **Integration Marketplace**: 25% commission on premium integrations
- **AI Credit Packs**: Direct sales at $25 for 5,000 credits

### 4. Partnership Revenue
- **Affiliate Program**: 10% commission on referred customers
- **Reseller Program**: 20% margin for reselling ClaudeFlare
- **Technology Partners**: 15% revenue share

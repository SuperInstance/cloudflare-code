# Project Name Selection: **NEXUS**

**Selected Name:** NEXUS - Distributed AI Coding Platform

**Final Selection Date:** 2026-01-13

---

## Why NEXUS?

### Meaning & Relevance

1. **"A connection or series of connections linking two or more things"**
   - Perfectly describes our distributed agent architecture
   - Represents the connection between:
     - Edge (Cloudflare) ↔ Local (GPU) ↔ Cloud (multiple providers)
     - User ↔ AI agents ↔ Code
     - Developers ↔ Their codebases ↔ AI assistance

2. **"The central and most important point or place"**
   - Positions the platform as the central hub for AI-powered development
   - Single entry point for all coding assistance needs

3. **Technical Precision**
   - Short (5 letters)
   - Memorable (two syllables: NEX-us)
   - Easy to spell and pronounce globally
   - Professional yet innovative

### Linguistic Analysis

- **Latin Origin**: *nexus* (binding, joining)
- **Phonetics**: Strong "N" and "X" sounds (conveys innovation and strength)
- **Domain Availability**: `.ai`, `.dev`, `.io` TLDs available
- **GitHub**: `@nexus-ai` namespace available

## Name Alternatives Considered

### Runner-Up: FORGE
- **Why:** "Building/crafting code" metaphor
- **Pros:** Strong, active verb
- **Cons:** Common in tech (less distinctive)

### Third Place: SYNAPSE
- **Why:** Neural connection, learning
- **Pros:** Scientific, precise
- **Cons:** Harder to spell, less consumer-friendly

### Other Ideas:
- **Vertex** (Edge + peak) - Taken by AI company
- **Vector** (Embeddings, direction) - Common in AI
- **Cascade** (Multi-tier) - Too waterfall-esque
- **Pulse** (Real-time, alive) - A bit generic
- **Orbit** (Cloud, around) - Lack of strength
- **Alloy** (Multi-component) - Industrial feel
- **Spark** (Quick, energetic) - Too common
- **Drift** (Easy, cloud-like) - Too passive
- **Loom** (Weaving) - Too soft
